Identify the owner of a building

Step 1.Search on Cook County Recorder of Deeds
Cook County Recorder of Deeds click "Click "Search Public Records""

On the next screen enter the pin number

Click on the most current warranty deed link under Type Des. an image should show up but if it doesn't click on the 'view images' tab located on the right hand side.

Click on the most current warranty deed link under Type Des. an image should show up but if it doesn't click on the 'view images' tab located on the right hand side.

Look through the document to locate the GRANTEE (The new owner of the property) and the address of the property.
If the Grantee is an LLC do a further search online to locate the address and owner of the LLC.
Side Note: Not all Warranty deeds look the same so the location where you'll find the Grantee and the address may differ.
